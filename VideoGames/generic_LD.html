Level Design

To use the Unreal and Unity game engines, to create level designs for video games using optional mechanisms and, more importantly, to construct a layout for players to walk through in practice.
Planned out and developed three projects concerning dierent level designs in two engines to provide the player with walk through ability, some simple mechanisms, and game play. The greatest challenge was to finish the project on time as well as to design a non-complex level for players to experience interactions on the level.
